Privacy policy

diagrams.free (https://diagrams.free)

Last updated: 5 June 2026

Plain-language summary, not legal advice.

Summary

diagrams.free is a free drawing app that runs in your browser. Your diagrams are stored on your device by default. We do not run application servers that receive or store your canvas data — the site is static files on GitHub Pages. We use Google Analytics 4 for basic visit statistics. Optional Google sign-in is only for features you turn on (such as Drive backup).

Who operates this site

diagrams.free is maintained independently of excalidraw.com and Excalidraw+. For privacy questions or requests, contact support@diagrams.free.

Data stored on your device

When you use diagrams.free, the following may be stored locally in your browser:

  • Active canvas — your current drawing, app preferences, and embedded images (browser localStorage and IndexedDB).
  • My scenes (scene vault) — named snapshots you save in the vault (separate IndexedDB database on your device).
  • Shape libraries — libraries you install for offline use.

This data stays on your device unless you export a .excalidraw file, copy a share link, or use an optional sync feature. Clearing site data in your browser removes local storage for diagrams.free.

What we do not collect on our servers

Because there is no diagrams.free backend for drawings, we do not receive your canvas content, vault contents, or exports unless you deliberately send them somewhere else (for example by uploading to your own Google Drive or emailing a file). Messages you send through Contact us are handled by Google as described in the contact form section below.

Contact form

If you use Contact us in the app menu or Help dialog, you send your name, email address, subject, and message to a small Google Apps Script service we operate. That service uses Google Gmail to deliver your message to support@diagrams.free and to send you a short confirmation email. We use this only to respond to your request, not for advertising. Your drawing data is not included unless you type it into the message field. You can also email us directly at support@diagrams.free without using the form.

Voluntary donations (Stripe)

If you choose Support diagrams.free in the app menu, you are sent to Stripe to complete a voluntary one-time or monthly payment. Stripe processes your payment; we do not receive or store your card number on diagrams.free servers (there is no application server for drawings). Stripe’s Privacy Policy applies to checkout. You can manage or cancel monthly support through Stripe’s customer tools or receipts.

Google Analytics 4

We use Google Analytics 4 (Google LLC) to understand how the site is used — for example page views, approximate location (country/region), device type, and referral source. Google may set cookies or use similar technologies. Analytics data is processed by Google under the Google Privacy Policy.

Your drawings are not sent to Google Analytics. Custom events in the app are limited to coarse product actions, not diagram content. Examples include: opening the command palette, exporting an image or file, starting a new saved canvas, first use of the drawing surface, and app load metadata (version, embedded vs top-level page).

Cookie consent (EEA and United Kingdom)

If you are in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, we ask for your choice before enabling analytics cookies. You can Accept analytics or Reject using the banner at the bottom of the page. Your choice is stored in your browser (local storage) so we do not ask again on every visit.

Until you accept, analytics storage stays off for your region (Google Consent Mode). Visitors outside the EEA/UK are not shown the banner; analytics may run under default settings for those regions unless you block it in your browser.

To decide whether to show the banner, we perform a one-time country lookup via api.country.is (your IP is processed by that service to return a country code). If the lookup fails, we show the banner to be safe.

You can change your mind later by clearing site data for diagrams.free in your browser, which removes the stored consent choice and will show the banner again on your next visit (in the EEA/UK).

You can also limit analytics by blocking scripts from googletagmanager.com / google-analytics.com, or installing Google’s Analytics opt-out add-on (where available for your browser).

Google sign-in and Drive (optional)

If you choose to connect Google Drive, sign-in and file access are handled by Google under Google’s privacy policy. diagrams.free requests only the access needed to work with files the app creates or opens for you, such as backup and restore under a diagrams.free/ folder on your Drive.

Typical OAuth scopes include:

  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.file — access to files created or opened by the app
  • openid, email, profile — basic account info if shown in the UI

You can revoke access anytime in your Google Account permissions. Files on Drive remain under your Google account and sharing settings.

Share links

If you use a share feature that uploads a scene to Google Drive, anyone with the link you distribute may be able to open that file according to the sharing settings you set in Google Drive. diagrams.free does not host shared diagram files on its own servers.

Other third-party services

Loading diagrams.free may contact other providers, for example:

  • Google Fonts — web fonts (for example Nanum Pen Script) may be loaded from Google’s font CDN when you use the editor.
  • Shape library catalog — browsing or installing community libraries may fetch metadata from libraries.excalidraw.com and library files from raw.githubusercontent.com (Excalidraw’s public library repository). Only what you choose to install is stored locally.
  • GitHub Pages — hosts the static site files when you visit diagrams.free.

Each third party has its own privacy practices.

Cookies and similar technologies

diagrams.free itself does not set authentication cookies for a diagrams.free account (there is no account). We store your analytics consent choice in browser local storage. Google Analytics and Google sign-in may set cookies or use local storage as described above when those features load and when you have allowed them.

Retention

Data on your device persists until you clear browser storage or uninstall site data. Analytics retention is controlled by Google Analytics settings. Drive files follow Google’s retention until you delete them.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of personal data. For data held only on your device, you can clear site data in your browser. For Google Analytics or Google Drive data, use Google’s tools or contact us at support@diagrams.free and we will help where we can.

Children

diagrams.free is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this page from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do. Continued use of the site after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.
