Privacy policy

diagrams.free (https://diagrams.free)

Last updated: June 2026

Summary

diagrams.free runs entirely in your browser. Your drawings are stored locally on your device unless you explicitly export or sync them using a feature you choose.

Local storage

The editor saves your active canvas and preferences in browser storage (localStorage and IndexedDB). This data stays on your device and is not sent to diagrams.free servers because there are no application servers — the site is static hosting.

Google sign-in and Drive (when enabled)

If you connect Google Drive, OAuth and file access are handled by Google under Google's privacy policy. diagrams.free only requests access needed to read and write files in your Drive that the app creates (for example under a diagrams.free/ folder).

Analytics

diagrams.free uses Google Analytics 4 to collect anonymous usage statistics (for example page views and coarse location). Google may use cookies; see Google’s privacy policy. Drawings and vault data are not sent to Google Analytics.
